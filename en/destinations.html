Diving Destinations

Komodo

Indonesia
The strong currents in this area bring a good amount of pelagic. White tips, tunas, grays,...and of course mantas

Lembongan - Nusa Penida

Indonesia
Manta rays can be found all year long. From October to April high chances of Mola Mola.

Sipadan - Mabul

Malaysia - Sabah
The spectacular barracuda point never fails. Togheter with lots of black tips, white tips, turtles and chances of hammerheads

Malapascua

Philippines
Malapascua has the highest chances for encounters with the rare threser shark.

Koh Phi Phi

Thailand
Warm, calm waters that allow unexperinced divers to have a relaxing experience

Apo Island

Philippines
The remote location of Apo allows some peaceful dives in its clear water and beatiful underwater landscapes

Gran Canaria

Spain
The fresh atlantic waters offer a very rewarding experience for the reef divers

Bali - Tulamben

Indonesia
House to one of the most famous wrecks in the world, the USS liberty wreck. Accesible in an easy shore dive

Tenggol Island

Malaysia
This still to be discovered gem offers the best diving in peninsular Malaysia

Sharm el Sheikh

Egypt
The colourful reefs together with the incredible biodiversity won't disappoint you.
