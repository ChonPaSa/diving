Try Diving

First breaths underwater. Trying Scuba diving you will have a first contact with the equipment and basic diving theory. In a day with a Theory session, a Swimming Pool training session and one Sea Dive. You will experience another world wearing scuba equipment and diving to a maximum depth of 12m. See spectacular fish and corals that await for you in the underwater world.

A PADI certificate of completion is issued following your experience allowing you to dive for 1 year to a maximum depth of 12m with a PADI Pro.

But be warned, it’s addictive and once you try it you won’t want to stop.

Get certified

The PADI Open Water course is by far the most fun way to enter into Scuba diving, the student learns all the basic skills to be a safe and confident diver by gaining an international certification. Qualified to a depth of 18m, the student can now travel and dive anywhere in the world.

Theory : Online or in the class room, you will learn the diving theory necessary to be an open water diver.
Confined water sesions : Five sessions conducted in cofined water to learn the basic scuba diving skills
Open water dives : Four open water dives that will allow you to put in practice the skills learned during the confined water sessions as well as explore the dive sites

The course comes with a PADI manual, include equipment use, instructor and PADI certification card.

Continue your education

Take your knowledge to a higher level! Learn to navigate underwater, go deeper and explore the multiple possiblities of diving in diferent environments.

This course includes one deep dive to increase your depth limit to 30 m, and a navigation dive to improve your skills skills. You chose 3 more dives : Drift, Peak Performance Buoyancy, Boat, Fish ID, Search & Recovery, or Digital Underwater Photography, Wreck...

All courses come with a PADI manual, include equipment use, instructor and PADI certification card.
